## Oracle EBS Performance Optimization

**Problem:** Critical import process taking 6+ hours, blocking daily operations

**Solution:**

- Implemented intelligent caching layer

**Result:** 
- **90% improvement** - Reduced processing time from 6 hours to 30 minutes

- Reduced system resource consumption by 70%
